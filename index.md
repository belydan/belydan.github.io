## 📚 Overview
The AH170-Framework provides a comprehensive bilingual dataset in Malay and English. This dataset is designed to help evaluate decision-making, stop-loss rules, and risk trade-offs in large language models (LLMs). It serves researchers, developers, and organizations focused on AI governance and reasoning.
